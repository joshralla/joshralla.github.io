What's Ultimate?

Ultimate is a fast-paced, non-contact field sport. All you need to play is a disc, some cones, and a playing field. The goal of the game is to score the most points by progressing the disc from your team's endzone to your opponents, without moving around the field with the disc.

What's Reign?

Reign is a junior ultimate club based out of Richmond, BC. For players aged 13-18 years old, we have up to four teams each summer for every gender and skill level.

Formerly Richmond Savage, our Open team placed 4th at the Canadian Ultimate Championships in 2018, and our Womens team placed 9th in 2017.
