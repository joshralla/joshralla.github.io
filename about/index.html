About Us

History

Formerly Richmond Savage, Reign Ultimate has been an integral part of the local ultimate community since 20XX.

Add ancient history pre-2015 here

2015
‣ Touring: Savage Open & Savage Womens
‣ Regional: Exert Open.

2016
‣ Touring: Savage Open & Savage Womens
‣ Regional: Slapnut Mangoes Open.
‣ Savage Open placed 8th at CUC 2016 in Winnipeg, MB.

2017
‣ Touring: Savage Open & Savage Womens
‣ Regional: Strike Open & Strike Womens
‣ Savage Womens placed 8th at CUC 2018 in Ottawa, ON.

2018
‣ First season under the Vancouver Ultimate League.
‣ Touring: Savage Open & Savage Womens
‣ Regional 1: Strike Open.
‣ Regional 2: Reckless Open
‣ Some players from Reign Womens played with Vortex teams at CUC 2018 in Brampton, ON.

2019
‣ Program rebranded as Reign Ultimate.
‣ Touring: Reign Open & Reign Womens.
‣ Regional: Reverse Open.
‣ Reign Open placed 8th at CUC 2019 in Edmonton, AB.

2020
‣ Played under reduced gameplay due to the COVID-19 pandemic.
‣ Regional: Reign Open & Reign Womens.
