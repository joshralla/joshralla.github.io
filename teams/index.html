Our Teams

Based out of Richmond, BC, Reign Ulimate focuses on providing a positive environment that emphasizes respect, fair-play, teamwork, hard work, and commitment. By the end of the summer players will have a sense of accomplishment, significantly increased knowledge and skills, a new set of friends, and many fun memories.

Our season runs from June to August and includes tryouts, practises, and up to three tournaments. Our program offers two options for our players depending on skill level and goals:

Touring

Reign Jr Womens & Jr Open

For players who intend on competing nationally at the 2020 Canadian Ultimate Championships (CUC) in Brampton, ON.

Regional

Reckless Jr Womens & Jr Open

For players who intend on competing regionally at the Vortex Cup Invitational in Surrey, BC.
